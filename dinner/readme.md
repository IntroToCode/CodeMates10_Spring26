# Slow Cooker Hot Honey Chicken Bowls

## Description
A perfect balance of sweet and heat, this dish features tender shredded chicken glazed in a homemade hot honey sauce. It is served with roasted sweet potatoes and a tangy dill pickle coleslaw.

### Ingredients:
**Hot Honey Chicken:**
- [ ] 3 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- [ ] 1 tbsp crushed red pepper flakes
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 2 tbsp coconut sugar
- [ ] 1 cup honey
- [ ] 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- [ ] 1/4 cup avocado oil

**Roasted Sweet Potatoes:**
- [ ] 2 large sweet potatoes, cut into 1/4-inch rounds
- [ ] 3 tbsp avocado oil
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste

**Dill Pickle Coleslaw & Dressing:**
- [ ] 1 bag (14-16 oz) coleslaw mix
- [ ] 1.5 cups chopped dill pickles, diced small
- [ ] 1/2 cup red or sweet onion, finely diced
- [ ] 1/4 rounded cup mayo
- [ ] 1/4 cup dill pickle juice
- [ ] 1/2 tsp garlic powder

### Steps for preparation:
1. **Season:** Mix spices and rub over chicken in the slow cooker.
2. **Cook:** Add honey, vinegar, and oil. Cook on HIGH for 2–2.5 hours.
3. **Roast:** Bake sweet potato rounds at 425°F for 35–40 minutes.
4. **Slaw:** Whisk dressing ingredients and toss with cabbage and pickles. Chill for 20 mins.
5. **Shred:** Shred chicken, reserve 1 cup of sauce, and toss chicken back into the slow cooker.
6. **Assemble:** Layer greens, rice, potatoes, slaw, and chicken. Drizzle with extra sauce.

### Notes:
* Sub coconut sugar for brown sugar if needed.
